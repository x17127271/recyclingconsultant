About Deep Blue Consultants

Mission

Deep Blue Consultants is an independant consulting firm. We provide services to a diverse clientele in the transportation, consumer products, construction, materials, financial services, Internet services, manufacturing, petrochemical and waste management industries, as well as government and other public sectors.

Our chosen field of services includes the most difficult issues that challenge society today. These challenges require solutions that will stand the test of time. Our solutions must recognize the social, economic and environmental implications and balance among these. Recognizing these challenges, we are committed to deliver services and solutions that are the product of the best collaboration of all appropriate resources and expertise of the firm.

Level of expertise

At Deep Blue Consultants we are committed to providing our customers with a reliable, consistent, cost effective and environmentally friendly waste management service. Our motto "bins collected on time, every time" is at the heart of our business. We pride ourselves on our reliable collection service and we are committed to this level of service.

First-class customer service

We aim to offer a first class service to our customers and best value for money. We are committed to promoting sustainability by reducing the volume of waste going to landfill, and our goal is to dispose zero waste to landfill.

Our focus is centred around recycling and composting and by placing this at the heart of our business, we are leading the way to reduce the carbon footprint of both our company and customers.

Location

Our head office is located in Mayor Street, IFSC, Dublin and boasts a 9 acre facility, 7.5 acres of which is under roof. The site is designed to manage the ever increasing volumes of recyclable and compostable material being produced in Ireland. Our EPA licensed facility is currently the largest waste transfer station in Ireland and handles waste from all over the country.
